The CIA triad involves three major focal points. Confidentiality ensures only the right people can access the right things. Integrity ensures all data has not been corrupted or tampered with.
Availability ensures the data is readily available at all times regardless of attacks or events.
This app showcases confidentiality by printing text only if the name entered exists in the roles dictonary.
It also showcases RBAC by welcoming users based on the value of the name keys in the dictonary or their "role."
